# SecureVote - Quick Start Guide 🚀

## ⚡ Get Started in 60 Seconds

### Step 1: Open the Website
- Simply open `index.html` in any web browser
- Or use a local server (Python: `python -m http.server 8000`)

### Step 2: Create Your Account
1. Click "Get Started" on the landing page
2. Fill in your details:
   - First Name & Last Name
   - Email Address
   - Phone Number
   - Password (minimum 8 characters)
3. Agree to Terms & Conditions
4. Click "Create Account"

### Step 3: Verify Your Email
1. An OTP (One-Time Password) will be displayed
2. For demo, use: `000000`
3. Or enter the OTP shown in browser console
4. Click "Verify OTP"

### Step 4: Cast Your Vote
1. Review all 4 candidates
2. Read their platform/policies
3. Click "Vote" on your preferred candidate
4. Confirm your vote in the popup
5. Receive your Vote ID (save for records)

### Step 5: View Admin Dashboard (Optional)
1. Open `admin-dashboard.html`
2. See live statistics:
   - Total Voters
   - Total Votes Cast
   - Voting Rate
   - Real-time vote distribution
3. Manage voters and candidates
4. View detailed results

## 🐛 Troubleshooting

**Issue:** Page shows blank  
→ Solution: Enable JavaScript in browser

**Issue:** Animations not working  
→ Solution: Use updated browser (Chrome 90+)

**Issue:** Lost data/votes  
→ Solution: Don't clear browser cache, or create new account

**Issue:** OTP not appearing  
→ Solution: Check browser console (F12), or use `000000`
